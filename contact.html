Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with JustPlay?

Getting started with JustPlay is easy! Simply contact us through the form above or email us at info@justplay.com.co. We'll help you set up your account and guide you through the platform features.

What sports does JustPlay support?

JustPlay is designed to support a wide variety of sports and activities. Our platform is flexible and can accommodate most team and individual sports, from football and basketball to tennis and volleyball.

Is JustPlay free to use?

We offer both free and premium features. Contact us to learn more about our pricing plans and what's included in each tier.

Can I integrate JustPlay with my existing systems?

Yes! JustPlay provides comprehensive APIs for integration with existing systems. Our development team can help you with custom integrations and technical support.

How secure is my data on JustPlay?

Security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption, secure authentication systems, and follow best practices for data protection. Your information is safe with us.
