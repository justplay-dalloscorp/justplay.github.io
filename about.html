About JustPlay

Connecting the sports community through innovative technology and passionate development.

Our Mission

JustPlay is dedicated to revolutionizing the way people connect through sports. We believe that sports have the power to bring communities together, foster friendships, and create lasting memories. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive platform that makes it easier than ever for players, teams, and organizers to connect, participate, and excel in their favorite sports.

We envision a world where finding a game, joining a team, or organizing a tournament is as simple as a few clicks. By leveraging modern technology and understanding the needs of the sports community, we're building tools that enhance the athletic experience for everyone involved.

What We Do

JustPlay is a comprehensive sports platform that serves multiple stakeholders in the sports ecosystem:

For Players

  • Discover and join sports events in your area
  • Find teams that match your skill level and interests
  • Track your sports statistics and achievements
  • Connect with other players who share your passion
  • Participate in tournaments and competitions

For Teams

  • Manage team rosters and member roles
  • Coordinate team activities and events
  • Track team performance and statistics
  • Find and recruit new team members
  • Organize team practices and matches

For Organizers

  • Create and manage sports events and tournaments
  • Handle participant registration and management
  • Generate tournament brackets and schedules
  • Track results and maintain tournament records
  • Promote events to the sports community

Our Technology

JustPlay is built using modern, scalable technologies that ensure reliability, security, and performance:

Backend Architecture

  • Clean Architecture: Our codebase follows clean architecture principles with clear separation of concerns
  • Domain-Driven Design: Each service is organized around business domains (Events, Teams, Tournaments, Users, etc.)
  • Microservices: Modular services that can scale independently
  • Firebase Integration: Leveraging Google Firebase for authentication, database, and cloud functions

Security & Authentication

  • PKCE Authentication: Advanced OAuth 2.0 flow for secure authentication
  • JWT Tokens: Secure token-based authentication with short expiration times
  • Firebase Auth: Integration with Google's robust authentication system
  • OTP Verification: One-time password system for account verification

Database & Storage

  • Firestore: NoSQL database for flexible data storage
  • Real-time Updates: Live data synchronization across all clients
  • Scalable Architecture: Designed to handle growing user bases and data volumes

API & Integration

  • RESTful APIs: Well-documented APIs for all platform features
  • Cloud Functions: Serverless functions for backend processing
  • Third-party Integration: Ready for integration with external services
