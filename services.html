Our Services

Comprehensive sports platform services designed to enhance your athletic experience and community connections.

Event Management

Create, manage, and participate in sports events with our comprehensive event management system. Whether you're organizing a casual pick-up game or a structured tournament, JustPlay provides all the tools you need.

Key Features:

  • Event Creation: Set up events with detailed information including sport type, location, date, time, and participant requirements
  • Capacity Management: Control the number of participants and manage waiting lists
  • Location Services: Find and share event locations with GPS integration
  • Real-time Updates: Keep participants informed with instant notifications and updates
  • Skill Level Matching: Match players based on skill level and experience
  • Equipment Requirements: Specify what equipment is needed for each event

Event Types Supported:

  • Casual matches and pick-up games
  • Practice sessions and training
  • Organized tournaments
  • Social sports meetups
  • League matches

Team Management

Build and manage your sports teams with our comprehensive team management system. From roster management to team statistics, keep your team organized and connected.

Key Features:

  • Member Management: Add and remove team members with role-based permissions
  • Role Assignment: Assign roles such as captain, coach, player, or manager
  • Team Statistics: Track team performance, wins, losses, and participation
  • Multi-team Support: Players can be members of multiple teams
  • Team History: Maintain a complete history of team activities and achievements
  • Communication Tools: Built-in messaging and notification system for team coordination

Team Roles:

  • Captain: Full team management permissions
  • Coach: Training and strategy management
  • Player: Standard team member with participation rights
  • Manager: Administrative and organizational responsibilities

Tournament Organization

Organize structured tournaments with our advanced tournament management system. From bracket generation to result tracking, manage every aspect of your tournament.

Key Features:

  • Bracket Generation: Automatic bracket creation for single or double elimination tournaments
  • Match Scheduling: Coordinate match times and locations
  • Result Tracking: Record and track match results in real-time
  • Public/Private Tournaments: Control tournament visibility and participation
  • Participant Management: Handle team and individual registrations
  • Prize Management: Set up and track tournament prizes and awards

Tournament Formats:

  • Single elimination brackets
  • Double elimination brackets
  • Round-robin tournaments
  • Swiss system tournaments
  • Custom tournament formats

User Profiles & Authentication

Secure user management with comprehensive profiles and authentication system. Keep your account safe while showcasing your sports achievements and preferences.

Key Features:

  • Secure Authentication: Google Firebase Auth integration with PKCE token validation
  • Comprehensive Profiles: Detailed user profiles with sports preferences and achievements
  • Social Media Integration: Connect your social media accounts
  • Activity History: Track your participation in events, teams, and tournaments
  • Statistics Dashboard: View your sports statistics and performance metrics
  • Privacy Controls: Control what information is visible to other users

Profile Information:

  • Personal information and contact details
  • Preferred sports and skill levels
  • Biography and sports background
  • Social media links
  • Profile picture and cover photo
  • Achievement badges and certifications

Additional Services

OTP Verification System

Secure one-time password system for account verification and password resets. Features include:

  • 6-digit random OTP generation
  • Email and SMS delivery options
  • Configurable expiration times
  • Attempt limit protection
  • Automatic cleanup of expired codes

PKCE Authentication

Advanced authentication system using Proof Key for Code Exchange (PKCE) for enhanced security:

  • JWT token-based authentication
  • Short-lived access tokens
  • Secure token validation
  • Cross-platform compatibility

API Integration

RESTful API services for third-party integrations and mobile applications:

  • Comprehensive API documentation
  • Rate limiting and security
  • Real-time data synchronization
  • Webhook support for events
